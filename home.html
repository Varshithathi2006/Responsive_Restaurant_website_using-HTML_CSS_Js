Kokoda

Kokoda is the national dish of Fiji. It is a ceviche of raw white fish like snapper, marinated in lime or lemon juice and mixed with diced tomatoes, chilies, onion, and coconut cream.

Pupusa

Pupusa is the national dish of El Salvador.These are thick corn tortillas filled with cheese and sometimes seasoned pork or loroco, a vine flower bud.
